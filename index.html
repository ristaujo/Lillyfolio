About Me

I'm a former journalist with a passion for writing, content strategy and trying new marketing tactics - the more unorthodox, the better. I embrace big-picture thinking and putting myself in the shoes of the client to provide creative and engaging solutions. Strong coffee, detailed to-do lists and writing on white boards are some of my favorite things.

I would love to work somewhere that allows me to merge my creative mindset and my passion for marketing every day. I enjoy working with people who challenge me to find interesting, engaging, data-driven solutions. When I'm not working, I can be found onstage with my improv comedy troupe, taking photos of my friends' dogs, or experimenting in the kitchen.
